The Midnight Library

Author: Matt Haig

Synopsis: In “The Midnight Library,” Matt Haig takes us on a profound journey through the infinite possibilities of life choices. Nora Seed finds herself in a library between life and death, where each book represents a different life she could have lived. The narrative beautifully explores themes of regret, hope, and the importance of choosing one’s own path.

Rating: ★★★★★
