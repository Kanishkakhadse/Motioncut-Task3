Project Hail Mary

Author: Andy Weir

Synopsis: Andy Weir's “Project Hail Mary” is a thrilling blend of science fiction and survival story. Following Ryland Grace, an astronaut who wakes up alone on a spaceship with no memory of his mission, Weir masterfully combines humor, tension, and intricate scientific details. The narrative keeps you on the edge of your seat as Grace uncovers his mission to save Earth from an impending disaster.

Rating: ★★★★☆
